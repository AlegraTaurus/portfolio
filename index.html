About me

I'm a passionate and experienced Art Director and designer, with a proven record across diverse industries, from FMCG, branding, homewares and apparel design to product development and packaging design. This has enabled me to development a holistic approach to design. I see design as a set of disciplines and a common approach to identifying people's needs and crafting elegant solutions to satisfy them. Effective design is not just about aesthetics or experience. It needs to consider how all aspects of a come together to deliver outstanding products and services that solve a customer need, while being sustainable to the business.
